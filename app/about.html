About us

Dear friends, Sushi and Quality lovers!

SUSHIPlanet has a simple mission: offering the best sushi
and unforgettable experience you can get in Dublin and surrounding areas of Co.Dublin.

All the scrumptious items on our menu are made from scratch and using only freshest seafood ingredients available and make our sushi using delicious, high quality, carefully hand-selected fish and seafood, including premium Tuna, Scottish Salmon, Eel, Octopus, Tiger Prawns, Crab, Scallops and more. Ever since our beginnings we have been working hard to make great sushi accessible, and deliver excellence straight to your door.

We offer 7 different types of Sushi for your choice: Nigiri, Gunkan, Hosomaki, Futomaki, Uramaki, Hot Maki and Tempura Maki. The different kinds of Sushi offer a wide range of tastes, and the main elements behind the taste are Wasabi and soy sauce. Use the soy sauce for dipping and mix the Wasabi with soy sauce to have a great taste of Sushi.

Selection of Sushi Sets and Combos are also available, Salads, Soups, Wok Noodles. Of course we did not forget about our sweet loversand have created yummiest Sweet Sushi ever. Selection of drinks are also available to quench thirst. Explore the menu!

We offer services such as Party, Weddings, Events, Chef hiring and more to accompany all your needs. For a lunch meeting, a festival or a business gathering Sushi is an excellent food decision to make. This nutritious snack is a satisfying and tasty treat for the audience.

These days more and more people are following a healthy lifestyleand our priority is your satisfaction. We are working hard to deliver the best service and to make sure all our food is perfectly balanced in each portion to deliver the best nutrition and unforgettable experience.

Sounds like a real feast for the senses? Discover SUSHIPlanet!

We are working for you!
